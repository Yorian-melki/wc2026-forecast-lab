# WC2026 dashboard glossary

This glossary does not change the model. It only makes the existing interface easier to publish and easier to read.

- **Group survival probability**: probability of reaching the knockout phase from the group stage.
- **Round of 16 probability**: probability of reaching the Round of 16.
- **Quarterfinal probability**: probability of reaching the quarterfinals.
- **Semifinal probability**: probability of reaching the semifinals.
- **Final probability**: probability of reaching the final.
- **Champion probability**: probability of winning the tournament.
- **Runner-up probability**: probability of losing the final.
- **Third-place probability**: probability of winning the third-place match.
- **Fourth-place probability**: probability of losing the third-place match.
- **Pairwise**: full matchup surface for all unique team pairs.
- **Fragility**: deterioration of tournament odds under adverse scenarios.
- **Upside**: improvement of tournament odds under favorable scenarios.
- **Path dependence**: sensitivity of tournament odds to the route through the bracket.
